About Five Star

GTA's Mobile Detailing Concierge

At Five Star Mobile Detailing, we bring premium car detailing services directly to your door — whether you're in Mississauga, Brampton, Oakville, or anywhere across the Greater Toronto Area. We arrive fully equipped with our own water and power, so you never need to visit a shop.

Since 2021 we've built our reputation one vehicle at a time on meticulous attention to detail, honest communication, and results that speak for themselves — from daily drivers to collector cars, corporate fleets to ceramic coating clients.

Concierge Convenience

Book online, tell us about your vehicle, and we'll come to you — at home, at the office, or wherever's easiest. No shop visit, no waiting room, no wasted time.
