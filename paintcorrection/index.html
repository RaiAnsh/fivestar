Paint Correction & Ceramic Coating

Paint Correction & Ceramic Coating

Our paint correction and ceramic coating services are the perfect combination to give your vehicle the ultimate protection and shine.

Paint correction removes imperfections from the paint surface — swirls, light scratches & oxidation — restoring a deep, reflective finish. Ceramic coating then creates a durable protective layer that repels dirt, grime & UV rays, keeping that finish locked in for years.

This combination ensures your vehicle stays looking like new for years to come. Contact us today to schedule an appointment and give your vehicle the care it deserves.

Certified Installations

We use certified Nano-Brite & System X ceramic coatings, backed by a multi-stage machine polish process tailored to your paint's condition — from a single-stage enhancement to a full multi-stage correction before coating.
